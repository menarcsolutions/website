## Bloated Templates vs Custom Code

Most agencies in Chennai charge you ₹30,000 to download a pre-built WordPress theme, change the logo, and hand it over. 

It is like paying a mechanic to fix your car, and they just spray-paint the hood and leave the engine sputtering. 

These template sites are loaded with plugin grease. They load slowly, break during updates, and get hacked easily because of database flaws. 

A professional web development company builds custom static sites using modern frameworks (like React or Next.js). The code is written from scratch. There is no database for hackers to exploit, no plugins to break on a Sunday night, and the load speed is instant.
