## The hidden fees the other guy forgets to mention

When you hire a web development services provider, make sure they outline the recurring costs. 

Nine times out of ten, cheap agencies hide their margins in the maintenance packages. They will sell you a cheap site, then charge you ₹3,000 a month to keep the server running. 

Here are the honest numbers you should plan for:

1.  **Domain Name:** ₹1,000 per year. (Never pay more for a standard `.com` or `.in` domain.)
2.  **Web Hosting:** ₹3,000 to ₹10,000 per year for standard business hosting. Custom static sites built on modern architectures like Vercel have zero hosting costs.
3.  **SSL Certificate:** Free. (If a web development agency tries to charge you ₹2,500 for a standard SSL certificate, hang up the phone. It is free on every modern server.)
4.  **Website Maintenance Packages:** ₹2,500 to ₹7,500 per month for backups, speed tuning, and security monitoring.

## Best practices for choosing a website design company in Chennai

Before you sign any contract, ask the agency these three questions:

*   **Will the website load in under 2 seconds?** Slow websites kill sales. Check their portfolio on Google PageSpeed. If their own site scores under 80, walk away.
*   **Do you offer flat-rate pricing?** Vague hourly pricing is how a ₹30,000 job becomes a ₹1,00,000 nightmare. Get the final number in writing.
*   **Do I own the code and domain?** Some agencies register your domain under their name. That is not business; it is a hostage situation. Ensure you own every asset.
