My neighbor recently bought a flat-pack plastic garden shed online. It arrived in a box, he clicked it together in about forty-five minutes, and he was thrilled. He put his lawnmower in it, and it works fine. 

But last week, he asked if we could run a commercial plumbing line into it to build an outdoor kitchen. 

I had to tell him the hard truth. You cannot run commercial lines through a thin sheet of plastic. If you want a kitchen, you need a concrete slab, structural timber, and proper copper pipes. 

AI website builders are the flat-pack plastic sheds of the internet. 

Let's look at the direct answer first.

**AI website builders are automated platforms that generate basic web pages from text prompts in under a minute, which are useful for drafting quick mockups but generate generic templates with slow code and lack the custom integrations needed for professional businesses.**

Here is the truth about the automated code machines.

---

## What is the hype? (A site in 60 seconds)

The marketing ads look incredible. "Describe your business in one sentence, and our AI will build your site."

You type: *I am a plumber in Chennai.*

Click. Sixty seconds later, the screen displays a homepage with generic stock photos of wrenches, a generic contact form, and copy that says "We leverage cutting-edge plumbing synergy." 

(I still do not know what that means, and I have been holding pipes for 22 years.)

---

## Why AI builders are plastic sheds

If your business only needs a simple page to show a logo and phone number, an AI site might get you by for a month. 

But if your website is your primary business engine, the plastic shed will collapse. Here is why:

*   **Bloated and Slow Code:** AI builders generate generic code blocks to cover every possible layout. This results in heavy files that slow down your mobile load speed. 
*   **The Duplicate Layout Problem:** The AI uses the same base logic for thousands of sites. Your site will look exactly like three other businesses in your local area. 
*   **Zero Custom Integrations:** If you want to connect your forms directly to your local database, sync bookings with your CRM, or trigger custom WhatsApp alerts, the AI tools cannot help. 
*   **No SEO Authority:** Search engines look for unique, high-quality content and clean schemas. Copied AI copy and template structures do not rank.

---

## The subscription trap

Many AI builders offer cheap entry rates. But once you want to connect a custom domain, remove their branding logo, or add a lead form, they lock you into a monthly subscription plan. 

Over three years, you end up paying ₹75,000 in monthly fees for a template you do not even own. 

With custom website development, you pay a flat, upfront fee. You own the code. You own the files. And your hosting costs are zero.

## Best practices for AI web tools

If you want to use AI, do it smart:
*   **Use it for drafts:** Ask AI to generate ideas for your site outline. 
*   **Rewrite the copy:** Never copy-paste raw AI text. It sounds corporate, dry, and lacks your actual voice.
*   **Hire a professional:** Once you have your ideas, hire a professional web development company to write clean, fast code.
