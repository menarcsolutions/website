Choosing a web agency can feel like buying a used car from a salesman wearing sunglasses indoors. Everyone promises high performance, but you might end up with a lemon. 

Let's look at the direct answer first.

**To choose the right website development company in Chennai, check if they write custom code or modify cheap templates, ask for their PageSpeed scores, ensure they offer flat-rate pricing rather than open-ended hourly rates, and confirm that you will own the source code.**

Let's look at the screening process that saves you from buying a digital headache.

---

## The $900 for a Washer Story (Hourly vs Flat Rates)

Let's talk about pricing transparency. 

In the trade world, there is a classic story about the customer who got billed ₹50,000 for changing a simple tap washer because the plumber charged by the hour and 'ran into complications.' 

Vague hourly quotes are the same trap in web design. 

An agency says they charge ₹2,500 per hour for business website development. A month later, they send a bill for eighty hours because integrating your contact form was 'more complex than expected.' 

A professional website development company does not play these billing games. They provide a flat-rate quote in writing before writing a single line of code. You know the exact cost upfront, and there are no surprises.

---

## Ask About Their Architecture (The WordPress Trap)

When you interview a web development company in Chennai, ask them this question: "Will you build this with custom static code or modify a WordPress theme?" 

If they answer WordPress, ask why. 

Nine out of ten agencies use WordPress because it is cheap and easy for them. But they will charge you custom pricing anyway. You end up with a slow site loaded with plugin grease that needs constant security updates. 

If they offer custom website development using modern frameworks (like React or Next.js), you are in safe hands. You get a site built with clean code that loads instantly and has no database security loopholes.

---

## The PageSpeed Test (Verify Their Portfolio)

Do not trust a developer's word about performance. Run a test. 

Take three client websites from the agency's portfolio and paste their URLs into Google PageSpeed Insights. 

If those sites take more than 5 seconds to load or score below 80/100, that company does not know how to optimize code. If they cannot build a fast website for their other clients, they won't build one for you.

---

## Rule of Thumb

If an agency spends the first meeting talking about 'digital transformation paradigms' instead of showing you loading speeds and explaining code security, walk away. 

Choose a company that provides flat-rate pricing, writes custom static code, and guarantees fast mobile loading speeds.
