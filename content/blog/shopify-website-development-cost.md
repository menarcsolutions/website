If you are setting up an online store, Shopify is probably the first name you heard. It is the big player in e-commerce. 

But a lot of agencies will quote you a low setup price and conveniently forget to mention the monthly app invoices. 

That is like buying a work van and forgetting to check the monthly finance rates and fuel costs. 

Let's look at the direct answer first.

**The average Shopify website development cost in India starts from ₹45,000 to ₹90,000 for standard store configurations, while custom-designed headless Shopify stores start from ₹1,50,000 flat, excluding recurring monthly platform subscriptions and app fees.**

Let's open the ledger and look at the true cost of running a Shopify store.

---

## The Base Setup Fee (The Purchase Price)

A professional web development company in Chennai charges an upfront fee to design and configure your Shopify store. 

This fee covers:
*   **Theme Curation:** Installing and customizing a premium Shopify theme to match your brand colors.
*   **Product Upload:** Setting up the catalog, product options (sizes/colors), and categories.
*   **Gateway Connections:** Integrating local payment processors (like Razorpay).
*   **Shipping & Tax Setup:** Configuring tax rules and courier rates.

For a standard store with under 100 products, expect to pay between ₹45,000 and ₹90,000 flat in developer fees. 

---

## The Shopify Tax (Monthly Subscriptions)

Unlike open-source platforms, Shopify is a closed system. You do not own the server space; you rent it.

*   **Basic Plan (₹2,500/month):** Best for small businesses. Covers 2 staff accounts and basic reports.
*   **Shopify Plan (₹6,500/month):** Best for growing brands. Adds professional reports and lower transaction rates.
*   **Advanced Plan (₹25,000/month):** Best for high-volume stores. Adds custom reports and third-party calculated shipping rates.

If you stop paying the monthly rent, your shop shuts down instantly. (There is no warning; the virtual landlord locks the doors.)

---

## The App Subscription Trap (The Extras)

This is where many business owners get caught out. 

Shopify's base system is actually quite simple. If you want advanced features like product bundles, wholesale pricing, custom invoice printing, or email marketing automation, you have to download third-party apps from their store. 

Most of these apps are not free. They charge recurring monthly subscriptions of ₹800 to ₹3,000. 

A client came to me because he was paying ₹30,000 a month in app subscriptions alone for a basic Shopify store. We rebuilt his site using custom website development connected to a headless Shopify backend, removing the app subscriptions entirely.

---

## Rule of Thumb

If you have a straightforward catalog of under 100 products and want to launch in two weeks without managing databases or hosting security, Shopify is a great option. Just plan for the recurring monthly subscription fees.
